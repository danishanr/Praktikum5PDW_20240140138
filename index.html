Artist: Yoshitomo Nara
Based in: Hirosaki, Japan
Recognized for: A leading Neo Pop figure since the 1990s. He is iconic for his "punk" children portraits that evoke a "naked emotional response," bridging the gap between high art and mass-produced imagery.
Artist: Hanna Kim
Based in: Busan, South Korea
Recognized for: Her work centers on the relationship between two characters, Hanna and Rabbit, which reflects her internal world. She has gained recognition through numerous solo exhibitions across Asia and wide-reaching collaborations.
